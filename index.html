Our Holistic Therapies

Acupuncture

Traditional needle therapy to balance energy, relieve pain, and support the body’s natural healing process.

Cupping Therapy

Gentle suction on the skin to release muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote deep relaxation.

Medical Yoga

Therapeutic yoga adapted to health conditions, helping posture, breathing, and nervous‑system balance.

Sound Therapy

Healing vibrations from singing bowls, tuning forks, or guided sound to calm the mind and reduce stress.

Bach Flower Remedy

Flower‑essence support for emotional balance, anxiety, grief, and mood swings.

Moxibation Therapy

Moxibation is a traditional Chinese medicine practice involving the burning of dried mugwort (moxa) near or on the skin to stimulate acupuncture points.

Magnet therapy

Magnet therapy, also called magneto therapy, applies magnetic fields to the body to promote healing, reduce pain, and improve circulation. It's a form of alternative medicine rooted in physiotherapy and traditional practices.

Auricular Therapy

Auriculotherapy is a complementary health modality, often called ear acupuncture or auricular therapy, that involves stimulating specific points on the external ear to treat physical and mental conditions.

Cosmetic Acupuncture

Cosmetic acupuncture, also known as facial rejuvenation acupuncture, is a holistic, non-surgical treatment that uses tiny, hair-thin needles to improve skin health and appearance.
